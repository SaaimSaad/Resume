Design Description

The Cropped Delta Wing design is a registered aerodynamic configuration developed for VTOL drone platforms. The design optimises the classic delta wing planform by cropping the leading-edge tips, reducing induced drag during hover-to-forward-flight transitions while maintaining structural rigidity and lift efficiency.

Academic

Research Paper

Cropped Delta Wing for VTOL Drone — Design Analysis

The accompanying research paper documents the aerodynamic analysis, design methodology, and performance evaluation of the cropped delta wing configuration. It covers lift-to-drag optimisation, stability modelling during VTOL transition phases, and structural integrity assessment of the wing geometry.
